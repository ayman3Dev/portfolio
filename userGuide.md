## Using Your Website

### 1. Navigating the Portfolio

The website features a clean, dark-mode design with neon accents. Use the navigation bar at the top to jump between sections:

- **Click "Home"** to return to the hero section with your introduction
- **Click "About"** to read your background, education, and experience
- **Click "Projects"** to view your featured software engineering projects
- **Click "Skills"** to see your technical expertise and core competencies
- **Click "Contact"** to reach out with inquiries or collaboration opportunities

### 2. Language Switching

Your portfolio supports both English and French. In the top navigation bar, you'll find language toggle buttons:

- **Click "EN"** to switch to English
- **Click "FR"** to switch to French

Your language preference is automatically saved in your browser.

### 3. Exploring Projects

The Projects section displays your best work in an interactive grid. Each project card shows:

- **Project name and date** - When you completed the work
- **Description** - A brief overview of what the project does
- **Skills used** - The technologies and concepts demonstrated
- **Hours invested** - Estimated workload for the project
- **Status badge** - Whether it passed or passed with bonus
- **"Code" button** - Links directly to the GitHub repository

Hover over any project card to see animated effects and highlights.

### 4. Viewing Skills

The Skills section is organized into three categories:

- **Languages** - Programming languages you're proficient in
- **Frameworks & Tools** - Libraries, platforms, and development tools
- **Core Concepts** - Fundamental computer science knowledge

Hover over any skill to see a glowing highlight effect.

### 5. Contacting You

The Contact section includes:

- **Contact form** - Fill in your name, email, and message, then click "Send Message"
- **Email address** - Direct mailto link to your email
- **Location** - Your current location (Khouribga, Morocco)
- **Social links** - Quick access to your GitHub and LinkedIn profiles

The form validates all fields before submission and provides instant feedback.

## Managing Your Website

### Updating Content

To make changes to your portfolio, use the **Management UI** accessible from the Chatbox:

- **Settings** - Update your website name, logo, and visibility settings
- **Domains** - Bind a custom domain or modify the auto-generated domain
- **Database** - View and manage contact form submissions and other data
- **Dashboard** - Monitor analytics, visitor statistics, and site performance

### Adding New Projects

To add a new project to your portfolio:

1. Contact Manus AI and request a new project entry
2. Provide the project name, description, skills, hours, and GitHub link
3. The AI will update the Projects section and deploy the changes

### Modifying Sections

Any section can be customized:

- **Hero Section** - Update your headline, subtitle, and introduction
- **About Section** - Modify your background, education, and experience details
- **Skills Section** - Add or remove skills from any category
- **Contact Section** - Update your email, location, or social links

Simply ask Manus AI to make these changes, and they'll be deployed immediately.
